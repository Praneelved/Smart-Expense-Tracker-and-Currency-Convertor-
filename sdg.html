🌍 Sustainable Development Goals

How this project supports the United Nations SDG framework for a better future.

SDG 8: Decent Work & Economic Growth

Promote sustained, inclusive economic growth and productive employment

How Our Project Contributes:
  • Financial Literacy — By tracking income and expenses, users develop better understanding of personal finance management.
  • Budget Planning — Encourages users to plan their spending, leading to better economic decisions at individual level.
  • Income Tracking — Helps users monitor their salary and earnings, promoting awareness of economic participation.
  • Currency Awareness — Understanding exchange rates helps in global economic participation and trade awareness.

SDG 12: Responsible Consumption

Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns

How Our Project Contributes:
  • Spending Awareness — Category-wise tracking makes users conscious of where their money goes, reducing impulsive spending.
  • Consumption Patterns — Visual breakdown of expenses helps identify areas of over-consumption.
  • Waste Reduction — When users see how much they spend on non-essentials, they tend to make more sustainable choices.
  • Mindful Spending — The remaining balance feature encourages users to think before every purchase.

🔗 How This Project Connects to SDGs

📊

When users categorize expenses into Food, Shopping, Travelling etc., they become aware of their consumption patterns. This awareness is the first step toward responsible spending.

📝 What are Sustainable Development Goals?

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are a set of 17 global goals adopted by the United Nations in 2015 as part of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development. They provide a shared blueprint for peace and prosperity for people and the planet. Each goal addresses specific global challenges including poverty, inequality, climate change, environmental degradation, and access to justice and education.

Our project specifically focuses on SDG 8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth) and SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production), as financial awareness and responsible spending are directly connected to these goals.
